PRESS SPACE TO START THE GAME:

This is a little user manual with the rules of the Game and control

Objective of the game is to take down all the alien spaceship hovering in space.

-You can move the spaceship around with left,right,up,down arrow key.
-Use that to collide with the alien ships.
-After you destroy all the ship, it will be gameover and you will get to see your scores in scorelist.txt
-Try to avoid asteroid as they will decrease your hp at very fast rate.
-if hp bar reachs zero, it's game over, take medkit, it'll refill you for one time.

Enjoy, i hope you like it. :)
